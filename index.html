About Us

The Vietnamese Student Association is a social and cultural oriented organization established back in 1989 at the University of North Carolina at Charlotte. We strive to promote Vietnamese culture and to encourage understanding amongst the UNC Charlotte community. Our main goal is to educate members and the UNC Charlotte community about the Vietnamese Culture. We exceed this goal through many cultural events and fundraisers. From learning the history of vietnam to delivering vietnamese delights across campus, we have connected many students together that turned into life long friendships. Every year, we host one of our biggest events known as Viet Night, which is a cultural showcase starred by many talented students, universities and local organizations. We are heavily dedicated to making sure that our show can leave an impactful mark onto the carolinian community as we attract many people from different cities within the Carolinas. We are more than just a club. We are a united family looking to influence vietnamese students and others to be a part of something special.
